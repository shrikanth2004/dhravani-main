Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how COILD DHRAVANI ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you participate in our speech corpus creation project for Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR).

Service Description

DHRAVANI is a web-based application developed under the "Center of Indian Language Data" project, designed for creating a speech corpus for ASR. The platform facilitates recording, managing, and organizing voice recordings with their corresponding transcriptions.

Information Collection

Voice Recordings

When you participate, your voice recordings are collected and stored. These may be used for research, development, and commercial purposes.

Demographic Information

  • Age group
  • Gender
  • Location
  • Accent information

Account Information

Google account authentication is required for participation.

Usage Instructions

  1. Sign in with your Google account
  2. Upload your transcript CSV file (csv header must be transcript or transcript_${lang_code})
  3. Select the language, location, gender, age group, and accent
  4. Click "Start Session" to begin recording
  5. Record by reading the transcript aloud
  6. Maximum recording duration is 30 seconds
  7. Option to re-record if audio is not properly captured
