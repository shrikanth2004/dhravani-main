Dhravani

Speech Corpus Creation Tool

Dhravani is a web-based application for creating a speech corpus for Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR). This tool helps in recording, managing, and organizing voice recordings with their corresponding transcriptions.

Developed as part of the funded project "Center of Indian Language Data"
